RULES OF PLAY

  • Max 5 people per group for the digital scorecard.
  • Max 6 strokes per hole.
  • Ball is out of bounds if it lands off the golf area of the hole being played. Place the ball back where it went out of bounds and take a 1 stroke penalty.
  • A player may move the ball 1 putter head in length away from all obstructions without improving their shot to the hole.
  • Do not interfere with play on other holes.

PLAY SAFE

  • Hit the ball gently.
  • Keep the putter head below your ankles at all times.
  • Do not run or jump on the course.
  • Do not climb on props or walls.
